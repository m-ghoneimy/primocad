Who we are

Primocad is your go-to hub for all things productivity and innovation in the AEC (Architecture, Engineering, and Construction) industry.


Behind Primocad is a dedicated team of industry experts, creative minds, and tech enthusiasts, all united by a shared passion for driving progress in the AEC field. We’re interested in technical software, and we’re here to support you every step of the way on your journey to success.


At Primocad, we’re passionate about empowering professionals like you – whether you’re an architect, surveyor, or technical enthusiast – with top-notch technical educational content and cutting-edge tools to boost your efficiency and creativity.


Our mission is simple: to provide you with high-quality resources, from tutorials and online courses to insightful ebooks, all designed to elevate your skills and streamline your workflow. We believe that by mastering the latest techniques and tools, you can unlock new levels of productivity and unleash your full potential.


When you join the Primocad community, you’re not just getting access to premium content and tools – you’re becoming part of a vibrant network of like-minded professionals who are as committed to your success as you are. We’re here to inspire, educate, and empower you to thrive in an ever-evolving industry.


Since 2018

In 2018, we launched our trusty app, Arrange. Since its first release, Arrange has amassed thousands of downloads and installations, earning widespread acclaim from professionals seeking to elevate precision in their design process. Recognized by the Autodesk App Store as a featured app multiple times, Arrange has captured the attention of industry writers like Heather Miller, a prominent content writer for the AutoCAD blog, who enthusiastically gave Arrange a well-deserved clout to the AutoCAD users.


"It’s one thing to position and move. But it can be challenging to arrange and align objects in AutoCAD, depending on the complexity. The Arrange app in the Autodesk App Store makes it a bit easier."

Heather Miller
AutoCAD Content Producer
